Pretty Little Liars' Troian Bellisario Expecting First Child With Husband Patrick J. Adams

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 27, 2024

Actress Troian Bellisario, who made a name for herself in Freeform's hit series Pretty Little Liars, is reportedly pregnant!

E! News reports that Bellisario and her husband, Meghan Markle's former on-screen Suits love interest Patrick J. Adams, are expecting their first child together. Currently, details about Bellisario's reported pregnancy are scarce. At the time of this writing, neither Bellisario nor Adams, who previously tied the knot in a glamping-themed wedding in December 2016, have publicly spoken out about their growing family.

However, news of Bellisario's pregnancy doesn't come completely out of left field. As E! News noted, fans speculated that Bellisario had a bun in the oven after she was photographed on vacation in Mykonos with Adams in late May 2018. In the paparazzi photos, Bellisario relaxed on the beach and waded into the water while donning a brown one-piece swimsuit, which appeared to be covering up a tiny baby bump.

Bellisario's Greek vacay came nearly two weeks after she and Adams attended Markle's wedding to Prince Harry on May 19. So it'd seem that Adams didn't just bring Bellisario as his plus-one to the grand event — he also may have brought along his future kiddo.

After leaving Suits after seven seasons on the show — his final episode aired in April 2018 — Adams remarked on the idea of focusing on his home life before embarking on the next stage of his career. "At the moment, I'm interested in taking a break. It's nice to be home. It's really nice to be with Troian, our dogs and to take the time to figure out exactly what shape I want to take my career after this," he told The Hollywood Reporter.

He later addressed the possibility of expanding their family at the premiere of Broken Star in July 2018. "We talk about it all the time, obviously, and when the time is right, the time will be right," Adams shared (via Entertainment Tonight). "You know, we're not going anywhere. Very much in love and excited for everything that comes next."

Seems like now is the right time for the happy couple. And, if certain rumors about Prince Harry and Meghan Markle are to be believed, the two former Suits stars could be raising kiddos at the very same time.
