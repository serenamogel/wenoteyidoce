White Wedding Of Comedian Woli Arole And His Wife (PHOTOS)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Sensational Nigerian comedian, Bayegun Oluwatoyin better known as Woli Arole had had his white wedding in grande-style and it was just lit.

The white wedding was held in Lagos on Saturday, April 10 and it was graced by top dignitaries in the movie industry, family, friends and fans.

Woli Arole and his lover looked blissful as they enjoyed the memorable moment in the life. Ghgossip gathered some stunning photos from the wedding.

The photos from the wedding wowed fans and social media users who chanced upon them. Just as it is done all the time, congratulatory messages and messages of good came in their numbers from social media users.

Alice Tony wrote; ‘Congratulations to them. May there home be bless with joy of marriage.’

Paul wrote; Beautiful, God bless your home

Cultured also wrote; Congrats to them

In a recent post, Bayegun Oluwatoyin better known was gifted a brand-new car on his wedding day. It was a very joyous and emotional moment was captured at the wedding when Woli Arole and his wife, Yemi was gifted a brand new Geely Emgrand EC7 GL car worth N5.6.

Don’t forget that the relationship of the lovely couple began in London and Woli Arole expressed his utmost joy to finally walk down the aisle with his lover.

Woli Arole, in October last year dressed like a mad man to create awareness on mental health and wellness where he threw light on the fact that some people look good outwardly but inward is a battle field hence the need to check up on others.
