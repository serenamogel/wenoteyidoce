As of Sunday, July 9, 2023, Rapinoe plays as a winger for OL Reign of the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL), as well as the United States national team.

Known for her crafty style of play, she’s the first player to score a goal directly from a corner at the Olympic Games, having done so twice.

Rapinoe played for the women’s national team at the 2011 FIFA Women’s World Cup where the U.S. finished in second place

She won gold with the team at the 2012 London Summer Olympics, the 2015 FIFA Women’s World Cup, and the 2019 FIFA Women’s World Cup.

During the 2012 London Olympics, she scored three goals and tallied a team-high four assists to lead the team to a gold medal

Rapinoe has had a stint with the Chicago Red Stars, Philadelphia Independence, and MagicJack in Women’s Professional Soccer (WPS).
