Just saw a preview of new Donna Summer Documentary

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 24, 2024

“Love to Love You, Baby” will premiere on HBO Max on May 20, but I went to a preview at a movie theatre that also featured a post show discussion with the Director (who also directed the Oscar-winning Summer of Soul Doc).

I think the Donna Summer doc is a masterpiece. She was an intensely private woman but her family handed over tons and tons of video she took of herself and her family through the years.

Big Donna fans may be a little disappointed that it’s not a chronicle of her songs and major musical events. It focuses more on her than just her music. It makes her 3-dimensional.

See it!!
