Cameron Mica Boyce (May 28, 1999–July 6, 2019) in Los Angeles, California, U.S. He was an American actor.

Career

He began his career in 2008, as a child actor. Boyce made his film debut in the horror film Mirrors (2008) and action thriller film Eagle Eye (2008). He has also worked in comedy films, including Grown Ups (2010) and its sequel Grown Ups 2 (2013). His first play role was on the Disney Channel Jessie (2011–2015, TV series).

He continued to work with Disney and achieved further prominence. Boyce starred as Carlos in the musical fantasy Descendants franchise (2015-2019, TV series), besides various spin-off media. He has also starred in the Gamer’s Guide to Pretty Much Everything (2015–2017, TV series).

The earliest of the third Descendants’ beginning in 2019, Boyce died at 20 because of complications from epilepsy. Posthumously, he features the thriller film Runt (2020) and was a regular in the supernatural thriller Paradise City (2021, TV series).

He lived in the Los Angeles area with his family. In May 2019, he moved in with former co-stars Karan Brar and Sophie Reynolds. He learned breakdancing, hip-hop, modern dance, jazz, tap dance, and ballet. His favorite style was breakdancing.

In 2012, he won the Young Artist Award for Best Performance in a Feature Film–Young Ensemble Cast. He also won the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Promotional Announcement. In 2018, he won the Pioneering Spirit Award for inspirational efforts to support the fight against the world’s water crisis.
