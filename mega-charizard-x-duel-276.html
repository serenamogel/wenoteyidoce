Mega Charizard X (Duel 276)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 3, 2024

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Mega Charizard X (Japanese: メガリザードンX Mega Lizardon X) is a figure in Pokémon Duel. It was made available in version 4.0.0. Shiny Mega Charizard X was made available in version 6.2.4.

Abilities

Inferno Ladder 煉獄の階梯
When this Pokémon appears as a Mega Evolution, remove the frozen condition from all of your Pokémon. Pokémon knocked out by the Attacks of this Pokémon are temporarily removed from the duel, returning to the bench 7 turns later. Your Fire-type Pokémon deal +10 damage and your Dragon-type Pokémon deal +10 damage.

Release information

Shiny Mega Charizard X

This figure was available for purchase in the Shiny Mega Charizard X Set from September 19 to October 1, 2018. During this time it was also available via Material Exchange for 5000 materials.

Gallery

Trivia

  • Prior to version 4.0.7, this figure had MP2.
  • Prior to version 7.0.14, Shiny Mega Charizard X was only able to be purchased in the Material Exchange when the Shiny Mega Charizard X Set was available in the Shop.

Origin

This figure is based on the X version of Charizard's Mega Evolution. Inferno Overdrive and Devastating Drake are Z-Moves in the games although Charizard cannot use them after Mega Evolving.
