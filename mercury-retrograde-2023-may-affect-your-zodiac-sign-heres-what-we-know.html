Mercury Retrograde 2023 may affect your zodiac sign. Here's what we know

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

The current Taurus season, which is usually a time associated with satisfaction, comfort, and stability, has been challenging to maintain due to back-to-back eclipses and a Mercury retrograde. The retrograde has caused confusion and uncertainty, rewriting what security looks like for every zodiac sign and making it difficult to relax and enjoy life's pleasures. However, as spring 2023's Mercury retrograde ends, every zodiac sign can expect groundbreaking epiphanies when it comes to establishing prolonged gratification.

The retrograde has brought forth a period of revision and reconsideration concerning materialistic and tangible things. All signs have been summoned to rethink the seemingly unmovable foundations and structures that have been in place for quite some time. This may have caused discomfort, but it also provides the opportunity to look at things from a different perspective. Security is meant to provide peace of mind, not stagnation. As Mercury begins to pick up speed, every sign can expect clarity in areas where things have been in limbo.

Here’s what every zodiac sign can expect as Mercury ends on May 14, 2023.

Aries (March 20 - April 20)

You've been thinking about financial security for the past three weeks, Aries, and as Mercury stations direct in Taurus, you'll finally have the understanding of the holdings and resources you've been seeking. The retrograde has given you the opportunity to get crystal clear on how you may establish this in new ways. You seek plenty of long-term comfort and consistency here. You'll be able to rationally decide how to properly preserve and keep your essentials when Mercury retrograde comes to a conclusion. It will give you opportunities to build more consistent income streams and improve financial stability.

Taurus (April 20 - May 21)

Although this month has been hectic for you, Taurus, you are now finally receiving some much-needed clarity on your desire for ongoing comfort and ease. You were compelled to think about your objectives for yourself and your reputation as Mercury retraced its path through your first house of identity. You know exactly how you want to present yourself to the world around you now that Mercury is moving forward once more.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Mercury, the planet that rules your horoscope, will end a lot of the inner dialogue you've been having with yourself. The fact that Mercury has been in the no-rush sign of Taurus since April 21 has pushed you to calm down some of your thoughts and ideas even though you are someone whose wheels are constantly turning. You can now proceed with whatever plans or initiatives you had to put on hold, though, as Mercury is once more gathering up speed. This will give clarity in relationships and partnerships, and a chance to reevaluate your deeper needs.

Cancer (June 20 - July 21)

Over the past few weeks, your community and friendships have undergone some restructuring. You'll have more clarity about the types of people you want to be with. Although this retrograde may have sparked some contentious discussions in your social circles, you can now move forward knowing exactly what you need from the people in your life to feel safe and fulfilled. A possible shift is expected in your daily routine that enhances your productivity and well-being.

Leo (July 21 - August 22)

This month, Mercury stations direct in your 10th house, beginning to clear up whatever uncertainty you may have been feeling about your career. Mercury has finally stationed direct, allowing you to proceed with some of your work-related goals that may have had to be put on wait in recent weeks. It should also be simpler to communicate with coworkers or colleagues now, so feel free to share your career-related strategies with those around you – they'll be listening! Mental clarity and creative solutions to any challenges that have been holding you back.

Virgo (August 22 - September 21)

The end of Mercury retrograde should provide you with some much-needed clarity if you have been confused about where you stand philosophically. The time is now for you to resume any studies in which you have shown interest or any travel plans that may have been delayed. Virgo, the world is your oyster, and with your natal ruler now direct, you can, at last, pursue the things that keep you informed without being constrained.

Libra (September 21 - October 22)

As Mercury stations direct in your eighth house, the revision you've been conducting to your shared resources and obligations is finally coming to a close. If you've been struggling to file taxes, sign contracts, or proceed with making a sizable purchase, these problems ought to be starting to ease up soon. You've spent the last few weeks organizing your affairs, so you should now feel more secure regarding your financial situation.

Scorpio (October 22 - November 21)

The end of Mercury Retrograde will give a sudden clarity to your partnerships and love relationships. You've been re-evaluating your relationships with those around you during the past three weeks, and now that Mercury is moving retrograde, you'll be better able to identify your needs in terms of how you interact with other people. Expect some of the misunderstandings that have been difficult to communicate with others to finally end today.

Sagittarius (November 21 - December 20)

The struggles you’ve been experiencing establishing a stable, comfortable routine will finally begin to subside. Now is the time to implement some of those habits and rituals that prioritize your well-being, Sagittarius — trust me, it’ll be much easier to do so now. Your ability to be productive and efficient will also slowly start to increase now — so be sure to tackle that post-Mercury retrograde to-do list.

Capricorn (December 20 - January 19)

Capricorn, feel free to continue any pastimes, relationships, or activities that have been stalled or uncertain. It's time to let your hair down right now. You will start reevaluating your financial security and discovering new ways to earn money or invest.

Aquarius (January 19 - February 20)

On May 14, when Mercury retrograde stations direct, you might have the strength to settle family disputes and establish the necessary limits to safeguard your energies. Dealing with domestic and family issues can be exhausting, but it's crucial to keep in mind that you have the power to build the house and family of your dreams. Your willingness to make a change is where it all starts.

Pisces (February 20 - March 20)

You might have the strength to settle family disputes and establish the necessary limits to safeguard your energies. Dealing with domestic and family issues can be exhausting, but it's crucial to keep in mind that you have the power to build the house and family of your dreams. Your willingness to make a change is where it all starts. There is a chance to redefine your relationship with the world around you, inspiring you to pursue your passions with renewed enthusiasm.
