Update on Roman Reigns next appearance in the WWE after the Crown Jewel Premium Live Event

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Roman Reigns will defend his Championship on this Saturday at the Crown Jewel Premium Live Event against Logan Paul. Reigns has been living his dream since the last two years and looks unbeatable at this stage of his title run.

Roman Reigns has built such a stable and gained the trust of his cousins in a way that they will always be a helping hand for him. Most of Reigns title defense have involved his cousins and they surely deserve the credit for his long tunning title run.

Roman Reigns’ most recent appearance in the WWE was on the 31st October edition of RAW, where he was warned by the Miz of Logan Paul’s big right hand and The Miz also mentioned how Paul had knocked out Jey Uso on one of the recent editions of SmackDown. Roman Reigns wasn’t clearly interested in The Miz’s talk and knocked him out with a Superman Punch. He further went on to say that everyone should be talking about him knocking Logan Paul out.  

The Tribal Chief’s current contract with the WWE states that he is allowed to appear at fewer WWE shows and not be a regular at the PLEs too. After the Crown Jewel PLE, Reigns is slated to appear at the SmackDown that will follow after the Crown Jewel.

The show will take place at the Gainbridge Fieldhouse, and along with Reigns many superstars like Braun Strowman, Ronda Rousey, The Usos and Drew McIntyre have been advertised for the episode. A dark match between Damian Priest and Matt Riddle will also take place.

Paul Heyman talks about Logan Paul beating Roman Reigns

Although the odds that Logan Paul will win against Roman Reigns are not that great, many wrestlers including Ronda Rousey, Jeff Jarret Dana Brooke are rooting for Paul to win at the event. Although Paul Heyman feels that Reigns will undoubtedly smash Logan Paul at the event, he has given his perspective on Paul winning at the event.

During a recent appearance on Corey Graves’ After the Bell Podcast, Paul Heyman said that Logan Paul won’t be a David Arquette if he wins the title and wouldn’t be a disgrace. Paul stated that Logan takes this business seriously and that if by chance Reigns loses and Logan Paul wins then he would be a worthy Champion.

“Whether this is the end of Roman’s reign or not, that’s another story. But, if by quirk of fate and act of God and the sky’s have a malice towards The Tribal Chief and Logan Paul becomes the Undisputed Champion, he wouldn’t be an unworthy champion.”
