43 Quotes About Wasted Love

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 13, 2024

Having the ability to love no matter what can offer you the greatest gifts of passion and romance. These quotes about wasted love highlight the failures that define our life when we throw a good relationship away and remind us to hold on to the moments instead.

“A man who dares to waste one hour of time has not discovered the value of life.”

“A year from now you will wish you had started today.”

“Any time you have an opportunity to make a difference in this world and you don’t, then you are wasting your time on Earth.”

“Anyone who loves in the expectation of being loved in return is wasting their time.”

“Do not waste time trying to convince people who have their mind made up.”

“Doing a thing well is often a waste of time.”

“Doing something you’re not fully committed to is a colossal waste of time.”

“Don’t waste your time on revenge. Those who hurt you will eventually face their own karma.”

“For every minute you are angry you lose sixty seconds of happiness.”

“Half the time men think they are talking business, they are wasting time.”

“I do not want to waste any time. And if you are not working on important things, you are wasting time.”

“I feel like I’m wasting time if I repeat myself.”

“If time be of all things the most precious, wasting time must be the greatest prodigality.”

“If you are not doing what you love, you are wasting your time.”

“If you spend too much time thinking about a thing, you’ll never get it done.”

“If you’re wasting time having fun, you’re not wasting time.”

“If you’re wasting your time or your attention, then start investing it wisely.”

“I’m not gonna waste your time, so I wouldn’t expect you to waste my time.”

“It’s bad enough wasting time without killing it.”

“Never make someone a priority when all you are to them is an option.”

“No point in wasting time with false vanity when you possess the real thing.”

“Nothing is a waste of time if you use the experience wisely.”

“One of the very worst uses of time is to do something very well that need not to be done at all.”

“Short as life is, we make it still shorter by the careless waste of time.”

“Someone wasting your time is far worse than someone wasting your money.”

“Sometimes we waste too much time to think about someone who doesn’t even think about us for a second.”

“Stop wanting someone who doesn’t want you. You’re just wasting your time.”

“The best way to fill time is to waste it.”

“The biggest mistake you can make in life is to waste your time.”

“The time you enjoy wasting is not wasted time.”

“There is no waste of time in life like that of making explanations.”

“There’s no good way to waste your time. Wasting time is just wasting time.”

“There’s no reason to waste time trying to prove who you are to people that don’t even know who they are.”

“To think too long about doing a thing often becomes its undoing.”

“Wasting time is something that people do or feel all over the world, not just in Italy.”

“Wasting time with the wrong person is just time wasted.”

“We cannot waste time. We can only waste ourselves.”

“We waste time looking for the perfect lover, instead of creating the perfect love.”

“What fools call wasting time is most often the best investment.”

“When one pays a visit it is for the purpose of wasting other people’s time, not one’s own.”

“When you kill time, remember that it has no resurrection.”

“When you spend time worrying, you’re simply using your imagination to create things you don’t want.”

“Your time is way too valuable to be wasting on people that can’t accept who you are.”

Having love for yourself before giving your love away to another is the best way to create value and show appreciation in yourself.
