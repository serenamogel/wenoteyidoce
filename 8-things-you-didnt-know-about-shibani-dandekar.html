8 Things You Didnt Know About Shibani Dandekar

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, January 30, 2024

➡ Shibani Dandekar Biography

The stunning Indian-Australian singer, actress, anchor, and model, Shibani Dandekar. She started off her career as a TV anchor in American TV. Later, she moved to India and started hosting several Hindi television shows and events. Besides, she worked as a model and a singer as well. She is notable for her outstanding, extraordinary talents that make her set apart from others. Well, here are some things that we have shared with you to know better about her life’s twists and turns. Let’s have a look!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Shibani Dandekar

1- Her relationship with Farhan Akhtar

There was news speculated that the multi-skilled star Shibani Dandekar was in a relationship with the most popular Indian actor, filmmaker Farhan Akhtar.

2- Her popularity began with Namaste America

Shibani Dandekar earned fame and recognition after appearing in ‘Namaste America’. It was her television debut which was started in 2001.

3- She calls singing her first love

Shibani Dandekar has been a passionate girl since her childhood as she always wanted to become a successful actress and singer. She calls singing her first love.

4- The leisure-time activities she loves to do

In her leisure time, the actress, Shibani Dandekar loves spending time dancing and traveling. She believes that traveling gives her a chance to get exposure.

5- She faced criticism after posting a topless photo on her Instagram

In Feb 2018, the actress posted a topless photo on her Instagram account for which she faced a lot of criticism on social media.

6- She is a seafood lover

Shibani Dandekar is a seafood lover and loves eating fish and prawns. Among others, she likes cake, burgers, pizza, and chocolates.

7- She is a huge fan of Madhuri Dixit

The actress Shibani Dandekar is a huge fan of Madhuri Dixit who is a champ of acting, dancing, and expressions. Even her fellow stars also admire her talent.

8- She is a pet lover

The actress Shibani Dandekar is a pet lover. She has a pet dog and loves spending time with her dog. And also, she shares her photos with her pets on her social media accounts.
