    Rolo Pretzel Buttons - Just 3 Ingredients!

    Just 3 easy ingredients and 5 quick minutes are all you need for these super delicious Rolo Pretzel Buttons! Great for Christmas, the holiday season, game day, parties and more!!

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword rolo pretzel buttons Prep Time 3 minutes minutes Cook Time 2 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 24 candies Calories 37kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 24 pretzels mini or square
    • 24 Rolo candies unwrapped
    • 24 M&M's color of your choosing

    Instructions

    • Preheat the oven to 350 F.

    • Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

    • Lay half of the pretzels out and top with a Rolo candy.

    • Bake for 2 to 4 minutes or until Rolos have softened.

    • Remove the baking sheet from the oven and gently press an M&M into the top of each Rolo.

    • Chill for 10 minutes or until chocolate has set.

    • Store leftovers in an airtight container.
