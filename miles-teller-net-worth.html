Miles Teller | Career

Miles has accumulated his net worth of an impressive $15 million from his career in acting.

After starting his career with the movie “Rabbit Hole,” Miles appeared regularly in various other films.

He then appeared in films such as Footloose, The Spectacular Now, and Project X.

His critically acclaimed movie “Whiplash” earned him several nominations and awards for the Best Actor category.

The movie Whiplash won Academy Award for Best Editing, Best Sound Mixing, and Best Supporting Actor and was nominated for Best Picture and Best Adapted Screenplay.

After the massive success of the movie, Miles slowly started to gain fame and recognition.

He was then paid $4 million for his role in the movie Fantastic 4 and $2 million for Project X.
