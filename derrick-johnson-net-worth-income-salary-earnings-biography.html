Derrick Johnson Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, January 22, 2024

Derrick “DJ” O’Hara Johnson (born November 22, 1982) is an American football linebacker for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. He was born in Kansas City, Missouri (NFL). He was a collegiate football player for the University of Texas, where he received consensus All-American accolades on two separate occasions. He was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs in the first round (15th overall) in the 2005 NFL Draft, and he has been selected to three Pro Bowls.
