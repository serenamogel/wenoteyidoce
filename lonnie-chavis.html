Meet Lonnie Chavis, ''This Is Us'' Star Cast - Bio &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Lonnie Chavis is a child actor famous for his role in the comedy, drama, and romance TV series, This Is Us.

He was born on November 23, 2007, in Downey, California, U.S. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius. He holds American nationality and belongs to the All-American ethnicity. 

Let's explore the personal and professional life of The Thundermans actor, Chavis.

Wrote An Article Against Racism 

Did you realize that the This Is Us actor Chavis encountered racism in his early life? If you were unaware of this, allow us to notify you that in June 2020, Lonnie Chavis authored and published an essay titled "America Needs to Change" on his encounters with prejudice. 

He authored the essay to draw attention to the long-standing structural racism in America as well as police brutality against Black people. In addition, he added that this was one of the most frequently raised and unresolved global issues.

Chavis Was Nearly Kidnapped

Many terrifying incidents happen in a celebrity's life, and one has also happened in Bella and the Bulldogs actor Chavis's life. 

When he was eight years old, he was nearly kidnapped by a stranger from his home in Long Beach, California. 

According to Chavis, he was approached by two men wearing masks while he went to dump the trash. After that, the kidnapper knocked him down. Fortunately, he was able to escape from them and run away.  

Soon after his escape, he contacted his neighbor, who safely escorted him home. After that incident, his mother homeschooled him.

  • Chavis was also nominated for the ''BET Awards'' and the ''Gold Derby Awards.''

Career Overview

Lonnie Chavis was passionate about acting from an early age. When he was four years old, he pointed at the TV and asked his mom if he could also be there. Full filling the request of her beloved son, Najah Chavis started submitting him for modeling and commercial opportunities.

After a short period, he began giving auditions for various kid roles in movies and TV series. In 2014 when he was seven years old, he got his first role in a short drama movie, What Showers Bring. Following it, he again appeared in Tortoise as Trick or Treater.

After that, he appeared in several movies and TV series Supergirl, The 5th Quarter and Kevin Hart's Guide to Black History. Currently, he is portraying the role of Randall in the famous NBC TV series This Is Us.
